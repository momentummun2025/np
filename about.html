About Momentum MUN

Our Journey

2015

Momentum MUN was founded with a vision to create a platform for young leaders to engage in diplomatic dialogue.

2017

Our first international conference hosted delegates from 15 countries, establishing our global presence.

2019

Expanded to include specialized committees focusing on contemporary global issues.

2022

Launched virtual conferencing capabilities, reaching delegates from over 40 countries worldwide.

2023

Celebrated our 8th anniversary with record participation and the introduction of crisis committees.

Our Mission

To empower youth through experiential learning in diplomacy, international relations, and public speaking, fostering global citizenship.

Our Vision

A world where young leaders are equipped with the skills and mindset to address global challenges collaboratively.

Our Values

Diplomacy, Integrity, Leadership, Inclusivity, and Innovation guide everything we do at Momentum MUN.
